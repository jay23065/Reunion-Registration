## 🖊️ Filling out your registration

After you launch the application, you will see a form on your screen. Follow these steps to complete your registration:

1. Enter your full name in the first box.
2. Provide your graduation year to help organizers group alumni correctly.
3. Type your current email address. The system uses this to send your registration confirmation.
4. Input your mobile phone number. Use the country code if you live outside of the primary school region.
5. Check all your information for typos.
6. Click the Submit button at the bottom of the page.
7. Wait while the application processes your request. The screen will display a message once the submission reaches the committee.

Can I register on my phone? 
Yes. The form adjusts its layout to fit mobile screens, tablets, and desktop monitors automatically.

What if I make a mistake on the form? 
If you submit incorrect information, contact the reunion committee through their official email address. They can manually update your record in their master list.

Is this software safe? 
The tool uses industry standard protocols to send your registration. It performs only the task of sending your form contents to the designated school contact. It does not monitor your activity or access your private files.
